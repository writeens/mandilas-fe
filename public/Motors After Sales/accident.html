Accident Recovery

All cars purchased from Mandilas Motors will be benefit from our complimentary Mandilas Accident Recovery Offer. So, if you are involved in an accident and your vehicle can’t be driven, simply call the phone number below and we’ll come and take your vehicle to the nearest Mandilas Workshop and Paint Shop.

Here, one of our highly trained Technicians will assess the level of damage and with your consent; we can have your vehicle repaired and back on the road in a short time.

Most insurers allow you to specify where your vehicle is to be repaired even if they run an approved repairer scheme. By using a Mandilas Body and Paint Shop, you can be rest assured that your vehicle will be returned to you in pristine condition using only Genuine Parts and approved repair methods.
