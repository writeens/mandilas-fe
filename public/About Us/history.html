Mandilas History

Our History

Mandilas is a trading and services conglomerate providing a range of internationally recognised products.

The Motors Division of the company is one of the leading Toyota distributors with a large nationwide branch network, providing unrivalled geographic coverage for the supply and excellent sales service of vehicles. It also retails quality used Toyota vehicles with limited warranty backed by the Mandilas brand


The Air Conditioning Division also under the umbrella of Mandilas group is an authorised independent distributor of Carrier Air Conditioners in Nigeria; providing a range of internationally recognized products and services

Our Carrier Air Conditioning expertise spreads across various services like repairs, maintenance, installation and sales with professionally equipped and experienced staff. The entire range of Carrier equipment i.e. rooms units, splits system, packaged equipment, commercial and industrial, central installations and adequate genuine Carrier spare parts for all equipment are in stock and available for supply

The Air conditioning Division is now NIS ISO 9001:2008 certified. That is, we are committed to providing the highest quality of air conditioning services to our customers in Nigeria. We ensure effective implementation of the requirements of NIS ISO 9001:2008 standard, targeted towards our esteemed customers’ satisfaction
