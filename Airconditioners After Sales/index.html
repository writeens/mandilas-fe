After Sales Services

Air Conditioners

The Mandilas Carrier After-Sales Service Department offers the most comprehensive commercial HVAC/R service program in the industry.
Our technicians have the capacity and are equipped to service your rooftop units, compressors, and chillers. We also provide preventive maintenance solutions to keep your systems operating efficiently.
We can design, replace, or upgrade your equipment to optimize the performance of your facility, whether you have one building or multiple sites nationwide.

We can assess your facility’s energy needs, and install and service energy saving solutions including variable speed drives, and building automation and control systems. Please review the resources listed below for additional information, request a quote now, or ask for more information.

Spare Parts

Mandilas Service department can provide the maintenance solution that meets your needs. Even if you have a dedicated maintenance team, Mandilas can help you find the right parts at reasonable prices, and assist when you need help with installation. Don’t put a high-performance system at risk by buying substitute parts.

Mandilas helps you locate the parts you need to keep your system running correctly:

• HVAC parts, including parts for chillers and rooftop units
• Building control system parts
• Replacement components, including compressors and motors
• Key OEM components
• Oil and refrigerant

Special Contract

The Contracts Division acts both to complement the Engineering Sales and as a stand-alone contracting section. The division provides the necessary agreements necessary for the installation of large Tertiary and Industrial central plant air conditioning systems throughout Nigeria.
